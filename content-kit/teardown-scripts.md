# 10 teardown scripts — hooks + beat sheets (faceless, 30–45s)

Fill `[STORE]`/`[NICHE]`/numbers from the actual store you audit. Hooks are the 80%.

---

**1. The surprise-shipping killer**
- HOOK: "This candle store loses a sale every 11 minutes at the exact same spot."
- Show product page → add to cart → checkout → shipping cost appears. Freeze. Circle it.
- "Nobody told them shipping was $6.95 until NOW. #1 abandonment reason in e-commerce."
- Fix: "Free shipping over $45, said right under the price. One sentence. That's it."
- Score → CTA.

**2. The vanishing buy button**
- HOOK: "Watch the most expensive scroll in Shopify."
- Screen-record mobile product page, scroll down… ATC gone. Count the scrolls back up.
- "71% of their traffic is on a phone doing THIS."
- Fix: "Sticky add-to-cart. It's literally a checkbox in their theme. 5 minutes."

**3. The 214 invisible reviews**
- HOOK: "This store has 214 five-star reviews. Their customers will never see them."
- Scroll product page — nothing near title… keep scrolling… reviews at the bottom.
- Fix: "Star badge next to the title, widget under the description. 15 minutes, free."

**4. 'Welcome to our store'**
- HOOK: "You can tell this store abandoned its own website in one screenshot."
- Zoom on default theme text ("Welcome to our store" / "Talk about your brand").
- "If YOU didn't finish your homepage, why would a stranger trust you with $40?"
- Fix: "One headline: what you sell, who it's for, why you. Five minutes of thinking."

**5. The popup that fires too soon**
- HOOK: "This store asks for your email before the page finishes loading."
- Record load → popup at 2 seconds → close it → "…and THAT's the bounce."
- Fix: "Exit intent or 30 seconds. Let people see the products before you propose."

**6. The coupon-hunt trap**
- HOOK: "This checkout sends its own buyers to Honey."
- Zoom the giant empty 'Discount code' box. "Empty box = 'other people pay less than you.'"
- "They leave, hunt a code, half never come back."
- Fix: "Collapse it to a link. Shopify setting. Two minutes."

**7. The missing bundle**
- HOOK: "This store sells a product people ALWAYS buy three of… one at a time."
- Show candles/soap/socks. "Their AOV is $28. One 'any 3 for $75' bundle: $75."
- Fix: "That's not an app. That's one product listing they never made."

**8. The 47-second speed run**
- HOOK: "Rating this store's checkout in real time. It has 47 seconds to take my money."
- Timer overlay. Add to cart → checkout → count every extra field/step. Stop timer at pain.
- "Forced account creation at second 31. Run ended."

**9. The glow-up (praise format — mix 1 in 5)**
- HOOK: "This tiny store out-converts brands 100x its size. Here's what they got right."
- Show 3 things done well, tied to checklist points. "Steal all three."
- (Praise posts get shared BY the store — free distribution to their audience.)

**10. The before/after math**
- HOOK: "3 free fixes = $1,100 a month for this store. Watch the math."
- Show sessions × CR × AOV on screen (calculator overlay), apply conservative fix ranges.
- "No new traffic. Same store. Three settings." → strongest CTA of the set.

---
Rotation: 1–8 problem formats · 9 praise · 10 math. Repeat formats with new stores/niches
forever — the format IS the show; the store is the episode.
