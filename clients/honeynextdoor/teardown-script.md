Finding #1 (neighborhood honey buried in a dropdown) turned into a faceless short.
~40 seconds. Screen-record honeynextdoor.com. This is a PRAISE-format teardown (see
content-kit/teardown-template.md, format #9) — it converts because the store may reshare it.

Bold, center-bottom, 2–5 words per beat. Keep the store's own warm palette; don't overlay
harsh red X's on a praise video — use a soft highlight/circle to point.
