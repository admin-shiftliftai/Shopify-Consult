# Shopify Conversion Checklist — 47 points, 8 areas

This is the audit's IP. Each point is scored **Pass / Weak / Fail / N/A**.
Area score = (Pass×2 + Weak×1) / (applicable points × 2) × 100.
Overall = weighted by the area weights below (weights reflect typical revenue impact).

## 1. First impression & hero (weight 15%)
1. Value proposition understandable in <5 seconds (what, for whom, why better)
2. Hero has ONE primary CTA above the fold (not competing CTAs)
3. Hero image/video shows product in use, not abstract branding
4. Page loads <3s on mobile (test via PageSpeed; hero not blocked by app scripts)
5. No auto-popup within first 10 seconds (email popups before value = bounce)
6. Announcement bar communicates offer/shipping threshold, not noise

## 2. Navigation & findability (weight 10%)
7. ≤7 top-level nav items; collections named by customer language, not internal jargon
8. Search visible in header (icon minimum; bar on stores >50 SKUs)
9. Best-sellers / "start here" collection exists and is linked from home
10. Footer has trust pages: shipping, returns, contact, about
11. Breadcrumbs on collection + product pages

## 3. Product page — the money page (weight 25%)
12. Title states the benefit or use-case, not just the product name
13. First 3 images: product alone → in use → scale/detail; zoomable
14. Price visible without scrolling; compare-at price shown when discounted
15. Add-to-cart button high-contrast, above the fold on mobile
16. Shipping cost/time expectation visible near ATC (before checkout surprise)
17. Description leads with benefits; specs collapsed below, scannable
18. Reviews visible with count + average near title (social proof placement)
19. At least one photo/UGC review on top sellers
20. Stock/urgency signals honest (no fake countdown timers — they destroy trust)
21. Size/variant selection can't be missed (no default-selected wrong variant)
22. Cross-sell ("pairs with") present but not cluttering the buy box
23. Return policy one click away from the buy box

## 4. Trust & credibility (weight 10%)
24. About page tells a real story with faces or origin (or deliberate brand persona)
25. Contact method visible (email/form/chat) — orphaned stores don't convert
26. Policies pages are real, not template lorem-ipsum defaults
27. Trust badges only where meaningful (payment icons at checkout, not spammed)
28. Professional favicon, no "Powered by Shopify" default in footer copy

## 5. Cart & checkout (weight 15%)
29. Cart accessible from every page; slide-out cart preferred over page redirect
30. Free-shipping progress bar in cart if threshold exists
31. Cart shows shipping estimate or explicit "calculated at checkout"
32. One-click express pay (Shop Pay / Apple Pay / PayPal) enabled
33. No forced account creation before checkout
34. Discount-code field doesn't invite "leave and hunt for coupons" (hide/inline it)
35. Order bump / cart upsell present (single, relevant, one-tap add)

## 6. Mobile experience (weight 10%)
36. Tap targets ≥44px; no horizontal scroll anywhere
37. Sticky add-to-cart on mobile product pages
38. Images sized/compressed for mobile (no 4MB heroes)
39. Fonts ≥16px body; no text-in-image for critical info

## 7. Offer & pricing psychology (weight 10%)
40. Bundles or volume discounts exist for consumable/multi-buy products
41. Free-shipping threshold set just above current AOV (drives basket growth)
42. Anchor product/tier exists (something premium that makes core look reasonable)
43. Guarantee stated plainly ("30-day money-back") near buy box

## 8. Retention & capture (weight 5%)
44. Email capture with real incentive, timed on exit-intent or 30s+ (not instant popup)
45. Post-purchase upsell or thank-you page offer configured
46. Abandoned-cart email flow active (ask merchant; check for Klaviyo/Shopify Email)
47. Order confirmation page reinforces brand + sets shipping expectation

## Scoring bands (for the report)

| Overall | Verdict |
|---------|---------|
| 85–100 | Optimized — focus on traffic + offer testing |
| 70–84 | Solid — a handful of high-impact leaks |
| 50–69 | Leaking — meaningful revenue lost daily |
| <50 | Critical — conversion fundamentals missing |

## Impact estimation rules (keep us honest)

- Use merchant's real numbers when provided (sessions × CR × AOV).
- Without analytics: state assumptions explicitly and give a RANGE.
- Attribute per-fix impact from published benchmark ranges (e.g., express pay
  +4–8% checkout completion; sticky mobile ATC +2–5% mobile CR), always cited
  as "typical range," never promised.
- Never sum best-cases into a fantasy total; report the conservative bound first.
