# Client intake form — Audit

Collect via Tally/Typeform (free tier fine), linked from the Stripe post-purchase page.
Every field below feeds Prompt 01 directly.

**Required**
1. Store URL
2. Your name + best email
3. What does your store sell, in one sentence?
4. Your #1 frustration right now (traffic? conversion? repeat buyers?)
5. Top 3 products by revenue (links)

**Strongly encouraged (better audit)**
6. Screenshot: last 30 days of Shopify Analytics overview (sessions, conversion rate, AOV)
7. Do you run email flows? (none / Shopify Email / Klaviyo / other)
8. Where does your traffic come from? (organic social / paid / SEO / other)
9. Anything you've already tried that didn't work?

**Consent line (include verbatim):**
"I understand ShiftLift may reference anonymized findings from this audit in marketing
content. My store will never be named without separate written permission."

> That last line quietly builds your teardown content pipeline from real client work —
> with consent handled upfront.
